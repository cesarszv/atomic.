Private habit tracking · Work in progress

A calmer way to keep routines visible.

ATOMIC is a private product in development for tracking habits, noticing patterns, and keeping personal progress clear without turning it into a feed.

Focus
Habits, routines, and steady consistency.
Quiet
No public feed, ranking, or performance pressure.
Status
Private access for the owner and team while the product takes shape.

The idea

Personal progress should feel understandable.

Track what matters

Keep daily and weekly habits visible without making the system louder than the routine.

Notice patterns

Return to progress with enough context to understand consistency, pauses, and momentum.

Stay out of the feed

Habit work stays private, practical, and separate from social comparison.

Principles

Built for a smaller, quieter loop.

  • Clarity before motivation theater.
  • Useful progress without public performance.
  • Personal ownership without heavy ceremony.
  • A focused product, not a productivity platform.

Access

Private while it is being shaped.

ATOMIC is not open for public signup. Access is limited to the owner and team while the product is refined.
